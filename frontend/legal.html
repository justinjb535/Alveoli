About STLS

STLS - Student Tracking & Ledger System is the first app under Alveoli Cloud.

Alveoli Cloud is a family of simple tools built for African schools and businesses.

With STLS, schools can:

  • Track Students and Attendance
  • Manage School Fees and Generate Invoices
  • Enter Marks and Print Report Cards in 1 Click
  • Communicate with Parents

Built to work partially offline and on any phone. No installation. No IT team needed.
