2. Student Registration

STLS handles automated tasks for you like Student IDs and student counts.

As the Head, Bursar, or Admin, you just enter: First Name, Middle Name, Surname, Grade, and Parent Details.

The system prevents duplicate entries. Once registered, each student gets their own Student Identification Number - SiD.

3.Fees Payment
